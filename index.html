Our Mission

KOMITS is a dynamic and innovative organization that has the customer as its primary focus. We specialize in design, import and distribution of quality business incentives, promotional products, and solutions. We work with our customers to better understand their desired outcome. We collaborate closely with manufacturers to resolve design and production issues, ensuring that the desired customized promotional products and solutions will arrive on time within the budget and according to quality specifications. Our mission is to be the Ultimate Promotional Product destination for. our customers. This will assist them in enhancing marketing campaign exposure and maximizing brand awareness by creating a unique identity for each client. We will do this by establishing and maintaining relationships based on mutual respect transparent communication, and benefit; by building a long lasting relationship with our stakeholders (customers and suppliers and by being open- compliance and ethics of minded discerning and service-oriented in our day to day operations
